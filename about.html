My software is mostly on GitHub. I'm currently CTO of AUX, where I'm building a team and platform to conduct auctions on the Ethereum blockchain, and recently finished my master's degree in computer science at Georgia Tech, where I specialized in machine learning. I enjoy writing Clojure, Idris, Ruby, Solidity, and Swift.
