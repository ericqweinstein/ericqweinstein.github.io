I write poems & programs. My poems have appeared or are forthcoming in The New Yorker, Ploughshares, The Yale Review, and others.

My software is mostly on GitHub. I've worked at startups, enterprise organizations, a consultancy, and even started my own company! I've done graduate work in creative writing (MFA, NYU) and computer science (MS, Georgia Tech). I'm currently Director, Engineering at HashiCorp, leading the Infrastructure Product Group (Terraform, Packer, Vagrant, and Waypoint).
