I write poems & programs. My poems have appeared or are forthcoming in AGNI, Alaska Quarterly Review, Barrow Street, The Believer, Crazyhorse, Gulf Coast, The Iowa Review, The New Yorker, Ploughshares, Shenandoah, The Southern Review, The Yale Review, and others.

My software is mostly on GitHub. I'm currently a senior engineer/lead at Hulu, where I work on the internal tools that power our advertising platform. Side projects include Bodega and CityShelf. I enjoy writing Clojure, Elixir, Idris, Ruby, and Swift.
