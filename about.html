I write poems & programs. My poems have appeared or are forthcoming in The New Yorker, Ploughshares, The Yale Review, and others.

My software is mostly on GitHub. I've worked at startups, enterprise organizations, a consultancy, and even started my own company! My graduate work is in creative writing (MFA, New York University) and computer science, with a concentration in machine learning (MS, Georgia Institute of Technology). I'm currently Director of Engineering at ZipRecruiter, where I'm helping build the world's first artificially intelligent job marketplace. I enjoy writing Clojure, Elixir, Idris, Ruby, and Swift.
